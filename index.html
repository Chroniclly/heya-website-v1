🎯 Our Focus Areas

Tackling the Issues That Matter Most

We leverage cutting-edge advocacy strategies and community-powered solutions to drive real, measurable change across three core pillars.

🏠

Attainable Housing

We push for inclusive zoning reform, rent stabilization, and public investment in affordable housing — ensuring young Americans aren't priced out of their communities.

📚

Equitable Education

Education funding shouldn't depend on your zip code. We fight for equitable resources, student debt relief pathways, and community-centered learning for all.

✊

Youth Civic Power

We build platforms for youth participation, community organizing, and direct engagement with the decision-makers shaping the world young people inherit.

💡 Core Values

What We Believe In

Our values aren't just words on a page — they're the foundation of everything we do, every decision we make, and every policy we fight for.

01

Housing is a Human Right 🏡

A safe, stable home is not a luxury. Every young person deserves housing that doesn't consume their entire livelihood — regardless of income or background.

02

Education Opens Every Door 🔑

Quality education shouldn't be determined by neighborhood wealth. Equitably funded public schools are the foundation for economic opportunity and upward mobility.

03

Youth Must Have a Seat at the Table 🪑

Policies that affect young people must be shaped by young people. Those most impacted know best — and we ensure their voices are heard in every room that matters.

04

Systemic Problems Need Systemic Solutions 🔧

Individual effort isn't enough when the system is broken. We fight for structural reform — not band-aids — in zoning law, school funding, and tenant protections.

05

Community Power Drives Change 💪

Real, lasting change comes from organized communities. We build coalitions and mobilize young people to show up where decisions are being made.

📋 Policy Platform

Our Six Demands

A comprehensive, evidence-backed policy framework designed to create systemic change and unlock opportunity for every young American.

Zoning Reform & Density Allowances

Eliminate single-family-only zoning in high-demand areas and allow multi-unit development near transit corridors.

Housing

First-Time Buyer & Renter Assistance

Expand federal and state down-payment assistance and emergency rental aid for young adults aged 18–30.

Housing

Equitable School Funding Formula

Replace property-tax-based school funding with state-level formulas ensuring equal baseline resources for all students.

Education

Student Debt Relief Pathways

Establish income-based forgiveness programs and expanded Pell Grant access so debt doesn't trap graduates before they start.

Education

Anti-Displacement & Tenant Protections

Pass just-cause eviction standards and rent stabilization to protect communities from gentrification-driven displacement.

Reform

Mandatory Youth Advisory Boards

Require youth advisory boards in local housing and education policy decisions for binding youth input.

Reform
